Welcome to Perspective!

This application enables a unique viewing experience for your favorite events! We combine multiple live views of an event and allow you to dynamically switch between offered views to give you the best perspective.



Supported key commands to control the active player:
  • [f]: Toggle full-screen display
  • [j]: Jump back 10 seconds
  • [k]: Play / Pause
  • [l]: Jump ahead 10 seconds
  • [m]: Mute or unmute
  • [0-9]: Jump to a point.
    0 jumps to the beginning,
    1 jumps to the time 10%,
    2 jumps to the time 20%,
    etc.
In addition, pressing the arrow keys jumps 5 seconds back (arrow left) or ahead (arrow right) in the current video.
